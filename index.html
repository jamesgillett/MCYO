EXPO MCYO @ Tête à Tête

MCYO are performing at the 2021 Tête à Tête festival, building on last year's 'Opera For Film' project, and bringing it into the physical world.

  • More

2020 — Opera for Film

As the arts confront the crisis brought about by COVID-19, Manchester Contemporary Youth Opera (MCYO) has launched a collaborative project, Opera for Film.

Two creative teams of young people aged 18–25 have created operas designed for screen rather than stage, available on MCYO youtube channel.

  • More

2019 — Duality

Six operas in 6000 seconds. These 6 operas were composed for performance in The Dancehouse, Manchester. Our performers worked closely with The Vonnegut Collective to create works of improvisation, music, movement, and more.
